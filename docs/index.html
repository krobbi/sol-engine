Controls

Press escape to stop the demo.
Hold "Q" to begin recording the average frames per second.
Release "Q" to display the recorded average frames per second in the console.
Use WASD or the arrow keys to move the Sol logo.
Press "E" or space to reset the position of the Sol logo.
